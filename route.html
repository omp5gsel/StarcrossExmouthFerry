The Starcross Ferry provides a link on the Exe Estuary Cycle Trail. It is a fairly flat route that allows you to travel a complete circuit of the estuary while enjoying its wonderful scenery, wildlife, towns, and villages.

Please bear in mind that the ferry can be busy during peak times.
Starcross Pier is accessed via steps over the railway bridge at Starcross, which may be unsuitable for wheelchairs. Trailers can be carried if folded, but only when space is available on board.
We regret that we are unable to pre-book spaces on the ferry.
